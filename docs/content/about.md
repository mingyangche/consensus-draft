# About WMDC Consensus

## What is WMDC?

The **World Meditation Day Consensus (WMDC)** is a collaborative platform designed to facilitate community-driven decision-making through meditation and mindful dialogue.

## Our Principles

1. **Collective Stillness** - We believe that quiet presence creates space for wisdom
2. **Inclusive Dialogue** - Every voice matters in our consensus process
3. **Gentle Progress** - We move at a pace that honors all participants
4. **Transparent Process** - All decisions and their rationale are openly documented

## How It Works

The WMDC consensus process involves several key stages:

1. **Intention Setting** - Clearly define the question or proposal
2. **Silent Reflection** - Each participant meditates on the matter
3. **Open Dialogue** - Share perspectives in a structured, respectful manner
4. **Sense Gathering** - Identify areas of agreement and concern
5. **Synthesis** - Create proposals that honor collective wisdom
6. **Consensus Confirmation** - Verify mutual acceptance

## Join Us

We welcome meditators, facilitators, and community builders from all backgrounds. Together, we can create more harmonious ways of making decisions.

> "In the stillness of collective meditation, we find the wisdom that transcends individual perspective."
