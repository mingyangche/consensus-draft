# Contact Us

We'd love to hear from you. Here are the best ways to reach us:

## GitHub

For technical issues, suggestions, and contributions:

- [Open an Issue](https://github.com/mingyangche/consensus-draft/issues)
- [Start a Discussion](https://github.com/mingyangche/consensus-draft/discussions)

## Community Channels

- **General Inquiries:** Open a GitHub Discussion
- **Consensus Questions:** Tag your post with `consensus`
- **Technical Support:** Tag your post with `technical`

## Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48-72 hours. Please understand that as volunteers, our response times may vary.

## Community Guidelines

When reaching out, please:

- Be respectful and constructive
- Search existing discussions before creating new ones
- Provide clear, detailed information
- Remain open to different perspectives
