# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

## Our Commitment

WMDC respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information.

## Information We Collect

### Public Information
- GitHub usernames (when you contribute)
- Content you voluntarily share in discussions
- Consensus participation records

### Technical Information
- Standard web server logs (IP addresses, browser type)
- Access times and pages visited

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Operate and improve our platform
- Facilitate consensus processes
- Communicate about decisions and updates
- Maintain community records

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

- When required by law
- To protect our rights
- With your explicit consent

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Request corrections
- Ask for deletion of non-essential data
- Opt out of non-essential communications

## Contact

For privacy concerns, please contact us through our [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/mingyangche/consensus-draft/issues).

---

*We believe in transparency and minimal data collection.*
