# Contribute to WMDC

We believe that meaningful contributions come in many forms. Here's how you can participate in building consensus.

## Ways to Contribute

### Share Your Perspective

Your unique viewpoint enriches our collective wisdom. Whether you're a seasoned meditator or just beginning your journey, your voice matters.

### Documentation

Help us capture the essence of our discussions and decisions. Good documentation ensures our consensus remains clear and accessible.

### Facilitation

If you're experienced in consensus processes or meditation facilitation, consider guiding sessions for others.

### Technical Contributions

- Website improvements
- Automation tools
- Data visualization
- Content management

## Getting Started

1. **Fork the Repository** - Create your own copy of our materials
2. **Join Discussions** - Participate in open conversations
3. **Propose Changes** - Submit suggestions through pull requests
4. **Review** - Help others by reviewing their contributions

## Code of Conduct

All participants agree to:

- Treat others with respect and kindness
- Practice active listening
- Approach disagreements with curiosity
- Support quiet reflection over heated debate
